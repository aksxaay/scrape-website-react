Designing with purpose

Many people underestimate the power of exceptional design. (Not you, of course, or you wouldn't be visiting our site.) The truth is that it takes more than a great product, a worthy cause, or extraordinary services to get noticed. 

In an ocean of tweets, likes, page views, and memes, we believe that a strategic, collaborative design approach best elevates organizations and their message.

Think of the world's most iconic and successful brands (you know who they are). They are easily recognizable, findable, and impactful. That's not by accident — visibility and trust are the superpowers of exceptional design.

Progressive organizations come to us for brand identities and digital platforms to set their ideas into action — and better the world. We never settle for mediocre (we don't even like writing that word), and neither should you.

We set the standard of exceptional design creatively, technically, sustainably, and ethically so that you can make the extraordinary impact impact that you seek. 

We’re ready to put some wind in your sails.

We deliver design with grace, integrity, honesty and vision—in other words, with purpose.

When you do important work and have bold goals to advance, you need a unique brand and website that turns heads, spurs action, and maybe even wins an award. But beyond the sparkle, it all needs to be durable, accessible, sustainable — and easy to maintain. 

We never compromise on delivering the whole package.

The company we keep

The organizations that seek us out embrace values that reflect a desire to contribute to a more just, equitable, and sustainable world.

B Corp proud

 

 

We are a Certified B Corporation, which requires a rigorous evaluation every three years to ensure we meet the highest social and environmental performance standards, accountability, and transparency. We scored 104.2 on the B Impact Assessment in April 2021.

 

 

 

Honoured to give back

We believe that there is a moral imperative to give back. In addition to our Purpose Project service grant for nonprofits and funding a scholarship for BIPOC design students, we donate to nonprofits that support environmental education and local community arts programming.
 

 

 

Striving to be inclusive

Manoverboard began as a design studio in pursuit of a more just and rational society. We remain committed to fostering a workplace and a set of partnerships that reflect the strength and diversity of the communities that we call home.

Committed to design

Everyone at Manoverboard strives to support and improve the design profession. Through our relationships with learning institutions, we teach and mentor students, and host internships. We are active in professional design associations and serve on local and national design organization boards and committees.

When you engage Manoverboard for your branding and digital platform work, our crew is your crew.

Ready, willing, and able

Although we admit to a fanatical obsession with design, we’re pretty normal otherwise, and our clients tell us we're remarkably approachable. (Whew!)

We are a spirited bunch with diverse thoughts and passions, but we all abide by the Manoverboard Code of Ethics and are committed to doing our best work for you while making the process totally enjoyable.
